FAQs

How long is the session?
The buildathon runs for one full day, from 8:00 AM to late evening, giving you plenty of time to build, learn, and connect.
What are the prizes?
There's $100,000 in total prizes including a $50,000 grand prize, $2,000 bounties for AI challenges, championship belts, and Aircoins for flash challenges.
What if I have to cancel?
Please let us know as soon as possible if you need to cancel. Cancellations made 7+ days before the event may be eligible for a refund.
Is the Buildathon right for me?
If you use Airtable and love to build — yes! Whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, there's a track for you.
Other questions?
Reach out to us at buildathon@airtable.com and we'll get back to you shortly.
